## Why we need you

We have a `sports-scientist` AI agent that's well-trained on evidence-based programming. But AI without expert feedback loop drifts.

You're our **outside check**. You validate our science, catch errors, push back on weak hypotheses, and lend credibility when we ship.

You're NOT a full-time employee. You're not committed to FORM as a career. You're committed to giving us 8-10 high-leverage hours per month.
