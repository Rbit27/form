## Our aesthetic (so you know if you fit)

**Yes:**
- Editorial typography з contrast between display serif (Fraunces) and clean sans (Manrope)
- Monospace для data, never proportional
- Warm near-black (#0B0A07) backgrounds, cream foreground, single acid accent (#DDFF2D)
- Grain texture overlay, ambient glow, generous whitespace
- Asymmetric and editorial layouts where they earn it
- Subtle, intentional motion

**No:**
- Inter as headline font
- Purple-to-blue gradients
- Stock photos of fitness models
- 3D rendered illustrations
- Pure flat-design uniformity
- Glassmorphism that doesn't earn its place
- Generic SaaS «Trusted by» logo rows

## The honest truth

1. **Visual identity is already there.** Our v0.4 marketing site is pretty polished. You're not starting blank canvas — you're inheriting and evolving.

2. **Product design is harder.** Mobile UX, edge cases, animations on real devices — this is where most of your work will live. The marketing site is the easy part.

3. **No design team yet.** You're sole designer for at least 6 months. Hard but high-leverage.

4. **You'll fight for things.** Founder is opinionated. So is `design-craft`. So is `brand-voice`. Healthy disagreements, but consensus-building takes effort.

5. **Compensation isn't FAANG.** Equity is meaningful but speculative. If you need cash >> equity, we're not the right fit.

6. **Brand discipline is non-negotiable.** Acid lime never covers > 10% of surface. No Inter as headline font. Ever. If this feels constraining, you'll struggle here.
