Camera data — architectural commitment

Raw camera frames and video are never uploaded to FORM's servers or any third party. Movement analysis runs entirely on-device (Apple Neural Engine or Android NPU). Only the derived form_score and rep_count are transmitted. This is an architectural commitment — no upload API exists for raw frames.

3.4 Food and Nutrition Logs Optional opt-in

Food and nutrition data is treated as health-adjacent special category data. You must separately opt in to the food logging feature. Nutrition data is never included in any enterprise dashboard or aggregate report.

3.5 Wearable and Biometric Data

Wearable data integration requires your explicit permission on both FORM and the wearable platform (HealthKit or Health Connect). You may revoke access at any time in your device's Health settings.

3.6 AI Coaching Conversation History

When you send a message to Victor, FORM assembles a coaching context and sends it to our AI inference provider (Anthropic). The context sent to Anthropic contains only pseudonymised exercise data — your sets, reps, RPE, programme phase, and equipment. Your name, email, age, weight, body metrics, and all other personal data are excluded from prompts. The Anthropic DPA prohibits use of FORM's data to train Anthropic's models.

3.7 Eating Disorder Awareness Screen

At onboarding, FORM conducts a brief non-diagnostic awareness screen. This cannot be skipped.

Clinical safety note: This is not a clinical diagnosis. Results are stored as a binary UX flag only — never as a clinical record, never included in any employer-visible report, never shared with any third party, never exported in identifiable form.

3.8 Device and Usage Data

Anonymous event data (screen views, feature interactions), crash reports (anonymised), and device/OS version. No health data values are included in analytics events. Legal basis: Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

3.9 Payment Metadata

FORM does not process payment card data. All payments are handled by Apple App Store and Google Play Store. FORM receives only: subscription tier, purchase date/expiry, and refund/cancellation status.

Section 5

How We Use Your Data

5.1 Core coaching and personalisation

We use your workout data, health profile, wearable data, food logs (if enabled), and coaching conversation history to generate personalised workout programmes, analyse movement quality, track training progress, adapt coaching intensity based on recovery signals (HRV, sleep, readiness), provide nutritional guidance (if food logging is enabled), and synthesise voice coaching cues.

Your health data is used exclusively to provide you with a better coaching experience. We do not use your health data for advertising, profiling unrelated to fitness, or any purpose beyond those stated in this Policy.

5.2 Service reliability and safety

We use anonymous usage events and crash reports to identify and fix bugs, monitor service uptime, detect unusual security-related activity, and ensure accuracy of coaching outputs. Health event content is never included in analytics events.

5.3 Enterprise deployments — privacy floor

These protections apply unconditionally as technical controls enforced at the database level, not merely policy commitments:

  • Your employer never sees your individual health data. No HR administrator, manager, or executive can access your individual workout records, body metrics, food logs, HRV data, coaching conversations, or eating disorder screening results.
  • Aggregate wellness data shown to employers is k-anonymised. Individual data is mathematically suppressed if a group contains fewer than five members. Body composition, nutrition, and eating disorder screening results are excluded from all employer-visible aggregates.
  • You can disconnect from your employer at any time via Settings → Privacy → "Disconnect from employer." Your personal health data remains yours.
  • No sharing with employer outside the k-anonymised dashboard. No exceptions. Enforced technically (database RLS policies) and contractually.

5.4 What we do not do

  • Sell your personal data or health data to any third party
  • Share your data with insurance companies, healthcare providers, or government agencies (except as required by law)
  • Use your data to train external AI models (Anthropic's enterprise DPA prohibits this)
  • Use your health data for advertising targeting, on or off the FORM platform
  • Share aggregate or de-identified wellness data with your employer that could enable individual re-identification
  • Use your eating disorder screening results for any purpose other than calibrating coaching language

6.2 Key data minimisation commitments

  • Anthropic receives no personally identifiable information. Prompts contain only pseudonymised exercise context. Validated in automated testing.
  • ElevenLabs receives no user data. Text-to-speech requests contain only the coaching text cue.

6.3 New sub-processors

6.4 Government and law enforcement requests

FORM does not provide backdoor access to any government or law enforcement agency. We will only disclose user data in response to a lawful court order or equivalent compulsory legal process, and only to the extent required. Where permitted by law, we will notify affected users before complying.

7.2 Account deletion and right to erasure

When you delete your FORM account (Settings → Profile → Delete Account):

  1. Your account is immediately marked for deletion and becomes inaccessible.
  2. A 30-day grace period begins. You may restore your account by contacting privacy@form.coach.
  3. After 30 days, all personal data in your user record is hard-deleted from our primary database.
  4. Backup copies are purged within 60 days of the deletion date (next backup rotation after the grace period).
  5. Audit log entries are anonymised: your user ID is removed; action type, timestamp, and system metadata are retained for audit integrity.
  6. You receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Total maximum retention after deletion request: 90 days (30-day grace + 60-day backup purge). After this, no data identifiable to you exists in FORM's systems or backups, except for anonymised audit log entries.

7.3 Data you can delete yourself

  • Individual workout sessions — delete from training history in the app
  • Food log entries — delete individual entries within the food log
  • Wearable data sync — revoke HealthKit / Health Connect access and delete previously synced data
  • All your data — Settings → Profile → Delete Account

Section 10

Children and Age Restrictions

FORM is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. By creating a FORM account, you confirm you are at least 16 years old.

For users between 16 and 18 years of age, FORM provides age-appropriate coaching parameters (intensity limits, exercise selection constraints) and excludes certain features that may be inappropriate for developing physiology. These constraints are applied automatically based on the date of birth you provide at onboarding.

If you believe that a person under 16 has provided personal data to FORM, contact privacy@form.coach immediately. We will delete any such data as quickly as practicable.

Section 11

International Data Transfers

FORM's infrastructure is primarily located in the United States, with EU data residency options available for enterprise customers.

11.1 Transfers to the United States

For transfers from the EEA, UK, or Ukraine to US-based processors, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) Module 2 (controller-to-processor). SCCs are in place with Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Cloudflare, Supabase, PostHog, and Stripe. Sentry SCCs are being finalised.

11.2 EU data residency option

Enterprise customers may elect to store their data in the EU (AWS eu-central-1 Frankfurt or eu-west-1 Ireland). With EU data residency: all database data is stored and processed within the EU; backups remain within the EU; Supabase EU and PostHog EU instances are used. Anthropic and Cloudflare remain US-based (SCCs in place). EU data residency is contractually bound at tenant creation and cannot be changed post-deployment without a full data migration engagement.

11.3 UK transfers

Transfers from the UK to US processors are covered by the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA), used in conjunction with SCC-equivalent addenda with our US sub-processors.

11.4 Ukrainian users

Transfers from Ukraine to processors outside Ukraine are subject to the Law of Ukraine on Personal Data Protection (Закон України «Про захист персональних даних»). FORM relies on contractual safeguards (DPAs and SCCs) with all processors receiving data from Ukrainian users.

Section 12

Analytics and Tracking

12.1 PostHog product analytics

FORM uses PostHog Inc to collect anonymous product analytics — which features are used, navigation patterns, where we can improve.

PostHog collects
  • Screen views and navigation events
  • Feature interaction events
  • Session duration
  • Device type and OS version (not linked to identity)
  • Analytics opt-out status
PostHog does NOT collect
  • Name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • Health data values (no form scores, body metrics, HRV, food log contents)
  • Coaching conversation content
  • Raw IP address (truncated at source)

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)). A Legitimate Interest Assessment has been completed.

Opt out: Settings → Privacy → Analytics → toggle to "Off". All PostHog event transmission stops immediately and persists across devices and reinstallations.

12.2 Sentry error monitoring

Sentry receives anonymised crash reports (error type, stack trace, app version, device type, anonymised session identifier). All health data fields are scrubbed at the SDK level before transmission. A formal DPA with Sentry is being finalised; SDK-level PII scrubbing is the compensating control.

12.3 No cross-site tracking or advertising trackers

FORM does not use advertising tracking pixels, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any cross-site tracking technology. We do not participate in any advertising network data sharing. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to any advertising or data broker platform.
