individual data.

Non-negotiable floors
Never HR sees individual user activity, workout, or health records

What HR & People leads do see

Opt-in aggregate signals that prove program value without exposing individuals.

Activation rate

▸
Opt-in NPS
Aggregated satisfaction score from employees who chose to participate in the survey.
▸
Seat utilisation
Contracted vs active seats for billing reconciliation. No per-user breakdown.

A deal that requires compromising employee privacy is not a deal we take.
