## Принцип

**Локалізація — це не translation.**

Це повна культурна адаптація product + voice + pricing + support + legal — для конкретного ринку.

Бо «приватний тренер» у Києві ≠ «персональный тренер» у Москві ≠ «personal trainer» у London ≠ «entrenador personal» у Mexico City.

## What "localization" includes

### 1. Translation (the easy part)

- Marketing site full localization
- App UI full localization
- App Store listing (title, subtitle, description, screenshots)
- Email templates
- Blog post translation (selective)
- Push notification copy

**Process:**
- Source language: English (primary)
- Translate to UA + new languages
- Reviewed by native speaker (paid contractor)
- Maintained у Lokalise or similar tool

### 2. Voice (Victor)

- ElevenLabs voice ID per language
- Cue library re-recorded для each language
- Tone calibration tested з native speakers
- Cultural-context adjustments (e.g., directness level varies)

### 3. Pricing

Already partially geo-priced:
- UA / EE: $9/mo
- Western EU / US / UK / CA / AU: $19/mo

Phase 1 additions:
- Poland: $11/mo (mid)
- Mexico / LatAm: $7/mo
- Germany: $19/mo (Western tier)

Considerations:
- Apple's geographic pricing tiers
- Local purchasing power
- Competitive landscape per market

### 4. Legal

Each new jurisdiction:
- Privacy Policy review (already EU-comprehensive)
- ToS review (governing law per market)
- App Store compliance per region

### 5. Support

- Native-language email support (contractor pool)
- Documentation localized
- FAQ localized

### 6. Content marketing

- Selective blog post translation
- Local creator partnerships
- Region-specific content (e.g., DE strength community has its own subculture)

## What we won't localize

### Right-to-left languages (RTL)

- Hebrew, Arabic — complex CSS/SwiftUI work
- Defer until market signals demand
- Estimated: 2028+ if ever

### CJK (Chinese, Japanese, Korean)

- Major translation + voice + culture work
- App Store CN restrictions complex
- Not on roadmap pre-Series B

### Tiny languages

- Languages з < 5M speakers — not commercially viable as standalone localization
- We'd offer English UI for these markets

## Engineering implications

### From day 1

- All copy in i18n-resource files (no hardcoded strings)
- Layout flexibility for text expansion (German text ~30% longer than English)
- Number / date / currency formatting through locale APIs
- Right-text-alignment ready (для future RTL)
- Date pickers and form inputs locale-aware
- HealthKit data uses metric/imperial appropriately

### Tools

- **Lokalise** or **Crowdin** для translation management
- **GitHub Actions** для auto-sync translations
- **Locale fallback chain** (e.g., DE-DE → DE → EN)

### Testing

- Pseudo-localization у dev (auto-expand strings, add accents)
- Native-speaker QA pass before each language ships
- Long-form post translation: not auto-MT, only professional

## Cultural calibration notes

### Ukrainian

- **Tone:** direct, slightly poetic, literary register encouraged
- **Forbidden:** Russian loanwords у казенному форматі; bureaucratese
- **Currency:** UAH primary; USD alternative
- **Conventions:** Imperial NOT used; metric only

### English (EN-US baseline)

- **Tone:** Hemingway-adjacent; no UK irony; no American hype
- **Forbidden:** sports-broadcast voice; «crushing it»; emoji
- **Currency:** USD
- **Conventions:** Imperial OR metric based on user setting

### English (EN-GB nuances)

- **Tone:** slightly more reserved than US
- **Forbidden:** Americanisms («color» → «colour», «liter» → «litre»)
- **Currency:** GBP
- **Conventions:** metric primarily

### German (Phase 1)

- **Tone:** precise, technical, no-bs (matches our brand naturally)
- **Pronoun:** «du» informal (fitness context)
- **Currency:** EUR
- **Conventions:** metric

### Polish (Phase 1)

- **Tone:** direct, similar to UA
- **Pronoun:** «ty» informal
- **Currency:** PLN
- **Conventions:** metric

### Spanish-LatAm (Phase 1)

- **Tone:** warm but professional; less directness than UA/EN
- **Pronoun:** «tú» informal (México) — adjust for other markets
- **Currency:** Multi-currency (MXN, ARS, COP, etc.)
- **Conventions:** metric

## Decision framework

Before adding a language, ask:

1. **Market size:** Is there 1M+ potential users у target persona?
2. **Demand signal:** Are we getting > 100 sign-ups/month from this geography with English UI?
3. **LTV match:** Will ARPU support our cost / margin?
4. **Cultural fit:** Does our brand voice translate without major rewrites?
5. **Maintenance commitment:** Can we sustain ongoing localization for 5+ years?

**3+ yeses = green light. < 3 = wait.**
