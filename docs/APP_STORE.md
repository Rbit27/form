## 1. Titles & Subtitles

### App Store Title

| Market | Text | Chars | Limit | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| UA | `FORM. Тренер у кишені` | 21 | 30 | OK |
| EN (US / Western EU) | `FORM. Your AI Lifting Coach` | 27 | 30 | OK |

**Notes on EN title:**
The marketing doc proposed `FORM. AI Trainer in Your Pocket` (31 chars — one over
the hard limit). `FORM. Your AI Lifting Coach` (27 chars) is within limit and is
more specific to the actual segment. "Lifting" signals who this is not for (casual
gym-goers, runners, yoga). "Your" does light personalization work without being
sycophantic. Do not use "Coach in Your Pocket" — too close to tagline, redundant
in search context.

- Харчування з повагою: лог без шейму, без компенсаторних механік, без food-policing

Для людей, які тренуються самостійно 1+ рік і хочуть знати — що саме робити сьогодні і чи правильно вони це роблять. Не для початківців. Не для тих, хто хоче «схуднути на літо».

**Пробний період:**

14 днів безкоштовно. Картка не потрібна. Після пробного: $9/місяць (UA) або $69/рік.

### EN · Full Description

---

A personal trainer in your pocket — without $200 a week.
Watches your technique through the camera. Adapts your plan to your sleep and HRV.
Victor's voice. No motivational noise. 14 days free, no card needed.

**What FORM does:**

- Builds a strength training program and adjusts it daily — not monthly — based on your recovery state
- Analyzes technique in real time through your phone camera on 8 foundational movements: squat, deadlift, bench, cable row, Romanian deadlift, overhead press, Bulgarian split squat, dumbbell row
- Reads HRV and sleep from Apple Watch, Whoop, Oura, or Garmin — and restructures your plan when your body hasn't recovered
- Victor, your coach: four tone options (smart-direct, mindful friend, cold professor, national-team coach). No motivational quotes.
- Nutrition without shame: logging without compensatory mechanics, without food-policing, without calorie-counting surveillance
- 90-day progress view: strength, PRs, consistency. No before/after photos.

**Who this is for:**

People who train independently with 1+ year of experience and want to know — exactly what to do today, and whether they're doing it right. Not for beginners. Not for people who want to "get in shape for summer."

**Safety:**

FORM is not a medical application. Onboarding includes a three-question ED screening. Any mention of pain routes through a clinical triage template: if it sounds serious, we recommend a doctor. No medical diagnoses. Cancel your subscription in 3 taps, no friction.

**Trial:**

14 days free. No card required. After trial: $19/month or $149/year (Western EU / US).

## 6. A/B Test Ideas — Screenshot 1 Headline Variants

Run each for minimum 1,000 impressions before calling a winner.

### Variant A (Control — shipped at launch)
```
UA: Твій тренер читає твої дані.
EN: Your coach reads your data.
```
**Frame:** Capability. Appeals to data-driven lifters.

### Variant B
```
UA: Програма, яка змінюється поки ти спиш.
EN: A program that changes while you sleep.
```
**Frame:** Automation. Broader appeal, not HRV-literate required.

### Variant C
```
UA: Тренер. Без $200 на тиждень.
EN: A trainer. Without the $200 a week.
```
**Frame:** Price anchor. Highest raw install volume risk — may attract lower-LTV users.
**Warning:** Do not optimize for installs alone. Monitor trial-to-paid. Abandon if trial-to-paid < 12%.

### Kill criteria
Abandon a variant after 2,000 impressions if:
- CTR < 3% (App Store fitness benchmark)
- Trial start rate > 20% below control
