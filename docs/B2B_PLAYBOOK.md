## Принцип

**B2B comes after PMF, not before.**

Багато стартапів падають у спокусу: B2B contracts feels like «real revenue» faster than consumer growth.

Реальність:
- Один corporate deal $50k looks great. But you spend 4 months selling, 6 months implementing.
- That same 10 months building B2C focus could land 3,000 paid subscribers at $20/mo = $720k ARR.
- B2B distracts from product-quality work, which is where moat is built.

**Wait until C-side metrics are healthy. Then add B2B as expansion, not replacement.**

- $6-8/seat/month negotiated
- Multi-year contract

### What's included
- All consumer features

### What's NOT included
- Individual user data access for employer
- Body comp / weight visibility to HR
- Manager-reports на employee fitness
- Compliance monitoring

## Sales process

### Discovery phase (week 0-2)

1. **Inbound or warm intro** to HR / People Lead / VP Operations
2. **15-min discovery call:**
   - Current wellness offerings?
   - What's working / not?
   - Budget cycle?
   - Decision makers?

### Demo phase (week 3-4)

1. **30-min demo з focus on:**
   - HR dashboard
   - Privacy guarantees
   - Real outcomes from consumer cohort
   - Pricing

2. **Take-home material:**
   - One-pager with ROI projections
   - Privacy / security overview
   - Implementation timeline

### Pilot phase (month 2-3)

1. **Optional 90-day pilot:**
   - 50 seats free for 90 days
   - Engagement metrics shared with employer
   - Success criteria pre-agreed
   - If hit → contract

### Contract phase (month 4-5)

1. **Standard MSA + DPA**
2. **Net-30 payment terms**
3. **Annual auto-renewal**
4. **30-day termination notice clause**

## What we promise companies

### Outcomes we'll commit to:

- Sign-up rate ≥ 30% of seats activated (industry baseline: 15-20%)
- Active usage rate ≥ 50% of activated weekly
- Engagement score (composite) ≥ 70/100
- Workplace stress score (self-report) improvement ≥ 10% over 12 months

### What we won't commit to:

- Specific health outcomes (weight loss, BP changes — too many variables)
- ROI in absolute dollar terms (varies wildly by industry)
- 100% adoption (we don't force users)

---

## What we promise employees

### Privacy non-negotiables

- HR sees aggregates only (% active, sessions averaged)
- HR cannot see your data
- HR cannot see who isn't using it
- You can revoke company association anytime (account moves to personal)

### Functional parity

- All consumer features available
- No reduced tier for B2B users
- All safety floors (ED, soft mode) preserved
- Your data is yours

### Easy off-ramp

- Leave the company? Account stays free для 6 months
- Or convert to personal subscription з 50% off lock-in for 12 months

## Anti-patterns we won't do

- ❌ Sell access to employee health data
- ❌ Provide individual-level fitness reports to HR
- ❌ Compete on «we have the most users» (vanity)
- ❌ Promise unrealistic outcome metrics
- ❌ Lock users into corporate-only accounts
- ❌ Tie performance reviews to FORM usage
- ❌ Provide insurance/health-record integrations без explicit consent
- ❌ Allow «fitness challenges» that publicize individual results

## Competition у B2B space

### Existing players

**Headspace for Work / Calm Business:**
- Strong on mental wellness, weak on fitness
- Wide adoption but low engagement on fitness side
- Our angle: «real fitness coaching, not meditation classes»

**Gympass / Wellhub:**
- Aggregator з gym reimbursement
- Doesn't coach
- Our angle: «we are the coaching layer above any gym»

**Whoop for Business:**
- Hardware-tied
- Cost per seat high ($30+ baseline)
- Our angle: «software-only, instant deploy, no hardware logistics»

**Self-built employee wellness:**
- Many enterprises have homegrown
- Most are clunky
- Our angle: «professional consumer-grade product, integrated»

### Where FORM wins

- Better unit economics (no hardware)
- Faster deployment (zero infrastructure)
- Higher engagement (we already proved consumer retention)
- Privacy-respecting (defaults, не disclaimers)

### Where FORM loses

- No mental health (Headspace fills that gap)
- No gym reimbursement (Gympass fills that)
- B2B brand recognition zero
- Sales team zero у year 1

**Strategy:** partner з Headspace-equivalent and Gympass-equivalent rather than compete head-on. We're the fitness-coaching layer, не «всеохоплююча wellness platform».

## Risks

1. **B2B distracts от B2C product quality.** Mitigation: cap B2B revenue at 30% total revenue until $2M+ ARR.

2. **Enterprise feature requests bloat product.** Mitigation: «no custom features < 100 seats» rule.

3. **Sales cycle longer than expected.** Mitigation: budget 6 months from intro to contract, не 3.

4. **Procurement-NDA delay launches.** Mitigation: standard MSA pre-approved by counsel.

5. **Privacy concerns block deals.** Mitigation: lead з privacy story у every deal.
