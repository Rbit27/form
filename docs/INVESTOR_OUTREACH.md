## 1 · Філософія

**Quality > quantity.** 30 thoughtful introductions to right investors > 300 cold templates.

**Warm intros first.** Cold-emails є lastresort, не starting point.

**Be selective who you pitch.** Investor — це 5-7 років partnership. Bad investor = pain. Скріни так, як вони скрінять тебе.

## 6 · Due diligence pack (what investors will ask for)

When investor takes follow-up call, prepare:

### Documents
- [ ] One-pager (PDF, 1 page)
- [ ] Pitch deck (10-12 slides, PDF — `docs/PITCH.md` rendered)
- [ ] Financial model (Excel, 3-year scenarios — `docs/FINANCIALS.md` rendered)
- [ ] Founder bio + resume (PDF)
- [ ] Cap table (Excel, current + post-money scenarios)
- [ ] Privacy & ToS drafts (`docs/LEGAL.md` rendered)
- [ ] Roadmap (Notion or similar, links to internal docs)
- [ ] Product video (2 min, screen capture of v0.2 prototype + voice-over)
- [ ] User research findings (если є — `docs/RESEARCH.md` synth)

### Reference call list

- 3-5 people who can speak to your character and capability
- Include former colleagues, founders you've helped, your manager
- Brief them: «expect call about FORM and me»

### Customer references (when have users)

- 5-10 power users willing to take a call
- Brief them: «honest answer, no script»

## 7 · Term sheet review (важливо)

If you get an offer — DON'T accept fast. Time is friend, not enemy.

Get an external review of:
- Liquidation preference (1x non-participating preferred — стандарт)
- Anti-dilution (broad-based weighted average — стандарт)
- Board composition (1 investor + 2 founders + 1 independent — healthy)
- Drag-along rights (standard, але read carefully)
- Pro-rata rights (give them, but cap)
- Information rights (monthly is OK, weekly is excessive)

**Red flags:**
- Liquidation > 1x or participating
- Board control to investor
- Vesting acceleration only on termination (without cause)
- Founder vesting > 4 years
- Anti-dilution = full ratchet

Have a startup-lawyer review. $1.5k now saves $1.5M later.

## 8 · How to handle "no"

Investor passes — 90% of the time it will. Don't take personally.

### Good "no" handling

```
Thanks for your time and direct response. Two questions if you have a moment:

1. What would have changed your mind here?
2. Is there anyone in your network who is more relevant for what we're building?

No pressure on either — I appreciate your time regardless.
```

**Rules:**
- Never argue with the no
- Never «can you reconsider»
- Ask for learnings (improve next pitch)
- Ask for intros (some investors будуть)
- Stay in touch monthly (3-5% will reconsider later)

### Bad "no" handling

- "Are you sure?"
- "Let me explain again why..."
- "Can you intro me to [10 other VCs]?" — спам
- "Why are you missing this opportunity?" — never
- Mass-blasting other VCs in response — never

## 9 · Common questions to expect

Prepare 60-sec answers to each:

1. "Why now?"
   → Apple Vision + LLM + wearables маturity converged in 2026.

2. "Why you?"
   → 10 років product engineering + personal training practice. Built the prototype solo. Have 14 specialized AI agents.

3. "Why won't Whoop / Apple do this?"
   → Whoop = hardware. Apple = consumer general. Neither moves at startup speed з coaching-focus.

4. "What's the moat?"
   → Brand voice (Victor persona + safety-floor). CV-data flywheel on 8 base lifts. Trust earned over time.

5. "What if CV doesn't work?"
   → Pivot to AI-program + HRV-aware coaching. $15-20M ARR business без CV. CV is upside.

6. "What's the bear case?"
   → Big incumbent ships «AI coach» with bundle distribution. We can't outspend, but can outbuild on dimensions they ignore (safety, honesty, voice).

7. "Why $1.5-2.5M і не $5M?"
   → Don't want growth pressure before PMF. Smaller raise = healthier decision-making.

8. "Why subscriptions, not freemium?"
   → MyFitnessPal model destroys LTV. Free 14-day trial з cancel-у-3-кліки = better signal.

9. "When can I see real numbers?"
   → TestFlight beta ships Q3 2026. Cohort data Q4 2026. Trial-to-paid M5 (Jan 2027).

10. "What's your founder skin in the game?"
    → [Personal answer — not template. Honest about runway, opportunity cost.]

## 10 · Anti-patterns

- **Spray-and-pray** — 1 email до 50 funds will get 1 reply
- **«Just want feedback»** — investors hate this; just pitch directly
- **Hidden ask** — be clear about raise size, terms, timeline
- **Over-claiming** — fake metrics are caught in 30 sec
- **Under-claiming** — selling yourself short doesn't help, just be honest
- **Following up 3x in week** — patience signals confidence
- **Negotiating against yourself** — let them anchor first
- **Comparing to competitors negatively** — speak about your strength
