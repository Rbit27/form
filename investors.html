§ 05 · Business model

Subscription.
No free tier.

Western EU / UK / US
$19 /month
or $149/year (−34%)

14-day free trial. Cancel in 3 clicks. No retention dark patterns.

Ukraine / Eastern Europe
$9 /month
or $69/year (−34%)

Geo-pricing preserves accessibility without destroying margin. Validated against local purchasing power.

Unit economics (target)
CAC (organic)$20–35
LTV (12-mo avg)$130–180
LTV / CAC4–7×
Gross margin78–84%

These are targets. Not results. No revenue exists yet.

Why no free tier

MyFitnessPal's model illustrates the problem: free tier users generate low LTV, drive support costs up, and dilute the product signal. A 14-day free trial removes commitment friction while preserving user intent — only people serious about training enter their payment details. This is a deliberate brand decision, not an oversight.
