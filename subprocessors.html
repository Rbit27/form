Privacy floor

No sub-processor listed below ever receives individual employee health data, CV keypoints, workout weights, or meal logs linked to an employer. All sub-processor data flows carry pseudonymous session identifiers only. The employer's Admin Dashboard surfaces aggregated engagement metrics (n ≥ 5 anonymity threshold) — never individual records. This is a hard architectural constraint, not a contractual promise.

Tier classification
T1 · Critical Primary data stores · SOC 2 in-scope

Processes health-adjacent or identity data directly. Annual SOC 2 report review + full Art. 28(3)(a)–(h) DPA + right to audit. An outage constitutes a platform-level incident.

T2 · Important Operational dependencies

Operational dependency; processes personal data but not Art. 9 health data directly. SOC 2 bridge letter or 8-item security questionnaire. Standard Art. 28(3) clauses + SCCs Module 2.

Change notification procedure

30 days
advance notice
— always.

Before we add or replace any T1 or T2 sub-processor, we notify all enterprise customers with active DPAs by in-app notification and email to the designated legal contact — at least 30 calendar days before the effective date.

If you object to a change within the 30-day window, email legal@form.coach with the subject line Sub-Processor Objection — [Company Name]. Silence within 30 days constitutes acceptance per your DPA Art. 28(3)(d).

What triggers a 30-day notice
▸Adding any new T1 or T2 sub-processor
▸Replacing a sub-processor with a functionally equivalent one
▸Changing the processing location or transfer mechanism for an existing sub-processor
▸Adding new data categories to an existing sub-processor's scope
What does not require a notice
—T3 / T4 vendor changes (no personal data processed)
—Removing a sub-processor (7-day informational notice only)
—Emergency replacements due to vendor breach — best-effort notice of ≥ 48h

Your rights
Right to object (Art. 28.2)

Enterprise customers may object to any new sub-processor within 30 days of the change notice. Email legal@form.coach.

Right to audit (Art. 28.3(h))

Annual SOC 2 Type II report available under NDA. On-site audits for Enterprise+ customers with ≥ 30 days' written notice.

Data subject rights (Art. 15–22)

FORM provides a /enterprise/v1/dsar API endpoint for data access, erasure, and portability requests. DSARs acknowledged within 72 hours; completed within 30 days.

Breach notification (Art. 33/34)

FORM notifies your designated legal contact within 8 hours of a confirmed breach affecting your tenant data. GDPR supervisory authority notification: 72 hours from FORM's awareness.

Data deletion on termination (Art. 28.3(g))

Request deletion or export via Admin Dashboard within 90 days of contract end. HMAC-chained audit log entries retained in access-controlled compliance vault for the legally required period (7 years), then irreversibly destroyed.
