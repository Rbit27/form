# Sample Strength Block · Week 1 / 6

> Це **демонстраційна** програма. У продукті FORM усе персоналізовано
> під твої дані. Тут — приклад того, як виглядає базовий тиждень для

---

## Контекст (припущення)

- **Користувач:** 28-35, 1-3 роки силового, без active injuries
- **Сила:** Squat 100-130кг, Bench 70-100, Deadlift 130-170
- **Recovery:** середній сон 7+, HRV stable
- **Equipment:** повний зал — barbell, rack, bench, plate-loaded
- **Goal:** збільшити силу на 3 базових + hypertrophy для естетики

## Блок дизайн

**6 тижнів:**
- Week 1-2 — Volume accumulation (база)
- Week 3-4 — Volume + intensity (top end)
- Week 5 — Peak intensity (1RM testing optional)
- Week 6 — Deload (60% volume, 80% intensity)

**Цей файл:** Week 1.

## Session 1 · Lower A (Squat-focus) · Monday

### Warm-up · 8 хв

- Bike або treadmill · 5 хв · pulse 110-130
- Hip mobility flow · 3 хв (90/90 hip switches, Cossack squats body weight)

### Main work

**1. Back squat · 4×6**
- Setup: barbell mid-traps або high-bar (personal preference)
- Tempo: 2-1-X-1 (2 sec descent, 1 sec pause at depth, explosive up, 1 sec at top)
- RPE target: 7-8 (couple of reps в запасі)
- Rest: 3 хв між сетами
- Weight: ~75-80% 1RM
- *Camera coaching:* кут спини, depth, knee valgus

**2. Romanian deadlift · 3×8**
- Setup: barbell from rack at hip-height
- Tempo: 3-0-1-0 (slow descent, no pause, fast up)
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 2.5 хв
- Weight: ~60% squat 1RM as starting point
- *Camera coaching:* hip hinge initiation, lumbar neutrality, bar path

**3. Bulgarian split squat · 3×10 per leg**
- Setup: rear foot elevated on bench, dumbbells in hands
- Tempo: 2-1-1-1
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 2 хв between legs (working through)
- Weight: 12-22 kg dumbbells starting
- *Camera not used* (single leg occluded)

**4. Standing calf raises · 3×15**
- Tempo: 1-2-1-0 (controlled, 2-sec hold at top)
- Rest: 60 sec
- Use seated machine або dumbbells

### Optional finisher · 4 хв

- Plank · 3×45 sec
- Side plank · 2×30 sec per side

### Cool-down · 3 хв

- Hip flexor stretch · 60 sec per side
- Hamstring stretch · 60 sec per side

**Total session time:** ~62 хв including transitions

**Total volume (sets):** 13 working sets

## Session 2 · Upper A (Push-focus) · Tuesday

### Warm-up · 8 хв

- Row machine · 5 хв · easy
- Shoulder mobility · 3 хв (band pass-throughs, scapular push-ups)

### Main work

**1. Bench press · 4×6**
- Tempo: 3-1-X-0 (slow descent, 1 sec pause on chest, explosive up)
- RPE: 7-8
- Rest: 3 хв
- Weight: ~75-80% 1RM
- *Camera coaching:* bar path, scapular retraction, elbow flare

**2. Overhead press · 3×8**
- Tempo: 2-0-1-1
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 2.5 хв
- Weight: ~50-55% squat 1RM
- *Camera coaching:* head position (chin tuck through bar path), lumbar extension

**3. Incline dumbbell press · 3×10**
- Setup: bench at 30°
- Tempo: 2-1-1-0
- RPE: 7-8
- Rest: 2 хв
- *Camera not used*

**4. Lateral raises · 3×12**
- Tempo: 2-0-1-1
- RPE: 8
- Rest: 60-75 sec
- Light dumbbells (5-10kg starting)

**5. Triceps pushdowns · 3×12**
- Cable, rope or straight bar
- Tempo: 2-0-1-1
- Rest: 60 sec

### Cool-down · 3 хв

- Chest stretch · 60 sec per side
- Triceps stretch · 30 sec per side

**Total:** ~58 хв
**Volume:** 16 working sets

## Session 3 · Rest або Zone 2 · Wednesday

- **If feeling tired:** full rest. Don't force it.

This is a recovery day, не «catch-up».

## Session 4 · Lower B (Deadlift-focus) · Thursday

### Warm-up · 10 хв

- Bike · 5 хв
- Hip mobility (90/90, hip airplanes) · 3 хв
- Empty bar deadlift practice · 2 хв (2 sets × 5 reps very light)

### Main work

**1. Conventional deadlift · 4×5**
- Tempo: X-0-X-0 (explosive both directions, brief pause at top)
- RPE: 7-8
- Rest: 3-4 хв
- Weight: ~75-80% 1RM
- *Camera coaching:* bar path, hip-shoulder synchrony, lumbar position

**2. Front squat · 3×6**
- Front rack position (Olympic clean grip або crossed-arm)
- Tempo: 3-1-1-0
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 2.5 хв
- Weight: ~65-70% of back squat 1RM
- *Camera coaching:* upright torso, elbow position

**3. Walking lunges · 3×10 per leg**
- Dumbbells in hands
- Tempo: 1-1-1-0
- Rest: 2 хв between sets
- Weight: 12-22kg per hand

**4. Hamstring curl · 3×12**
- Machine
- Tempo: 2-1-1-1
- RPE: 8
- Rest: 75 sec

### Cool-down · 4 хв

- Pigeon pose · 60 sec per side
- Cat-cow · 30 sec
- Foam roll back · 90 sec

**Total:** ~60 хв
**Volume:** 13 working sets

## Session 5 · Upper B (Pull-focus) · Friday

### Warm-up · 8 хв

- Row machine · 5 хв
- Shoulder mobility · 3 хв (band pulls, scapular CARs)

### Main work

**1. Pull-up або lat pulldown · 4×6**
- If bodyweight: weighted з vest або belt if doing pull-ups
- If pulldown: use long-handle wide grip
- Tempo: 2-1-1-1
- RPE: 7-8
- Rest: 2.5 хв
- *Camera coaching:* shoulder depression, lat engagement (pull-up only)

**2. Bent-over row (barbell) · 4×8**
- Hip hinge to ~45°
- Tempo: 2-1-1-0
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 2.5 хв
- Weight: ~50% bench press 1RM starting
- *Camera coaching:* hip position, elbow path, scapular retraction

**3. Face pulls (cable rope) · 3×15**
- Tempo: 2-1-1-1
- RPE: 7
- Rest: 60 sec
- Focus on rear delts + external rotation

**4. Barbell curl · 3×10**
- Tempo: 2-0-1-1
- RPE: 7-8
- Rest: 75 sec

**5. Cable rear delt fly · 3×12**
- Tempo: 2-1-1-1
- Rest: 60 sec

### Cool-down · 3 хв

- Lat stretch · 60 sec per side
- Doorway chest stretch · 60 sec

**Total:** ~58 хв
**Volume:** 17 working sets

## Adjustments by recovery

If FORM detects:

- **HRV down 15%+ for 2 consecutive days:** swap heavy session for accessory або move forward by 1 day
- **Sleep < 6h for 2 nights:** reduce volume by one set on each lift
- **Subjective rating < 15 (5-point scale):** propose A) move heavy day or B) replace with mobility
- **Pain rating ≥ 4/10:** stop the relevant movement, swap to alternative, recommend MD if persists

**These are AI-suggestions, не commands.** User confirms.
