# Sample Deload Week

> Deload — не «легкий тиждень». Це **structured stimulus reduction** з нагрузкою щоб recovery систем повноцінно catch up.
>
> Не пропускай deload. Це найважливіша частина прогресу.

---

## Що таке deload (швидко)

Після 4-6 тижнів стимулу:
- Накопичується **систем стрес** (CNS, joints, hormones)
- Зростання уповільнюється
- Risk травми зростає
- Sleep / HRV gradually degradeє

Deload = свідоме зниження stimulus, щоб тіло переробило накопичений stimulus у adaptation.

Не deload = stall → injury → forced rest.

---

## Принципи цього deload

1. **Об'єм -50%** (приблизно) від попереднього тижня
2. **Інтенсивність 70-75%** від working weights (НЕ 0%)
3. **Frequency 3 sessions** замість 4 (один день поза трасою)
4. **Tempo підкреслено slow** на ексцентриці
5. **Camera coaching активний** — best week для polishing техніки
6. **Mobility робота 2× частіше**
7. **Sleep priority** — не намагайся добити «потерянное»
8. **No 1RM testing** — week 5 буде це

## Session 1 · Lower light + Mobility · Monday

### Warm-up · 12 хв (longer than usual)

- Bike easy · 5 хв
- Hip mobility flow · 4 хв (90/90s, Cossack squats, hip airplanes)
- Empty bar squat practice · 2x10 з increasing focus on form

### Main work

**1. Back squat · 3×8 @ RPE 5**
- Weight ~65% 1RM (working weight × 0.75 cap)
- Tempo: 3-1-1-1 (slow eccentric, paused, normal up)
- Rest: 2 хв
- *Camera coaching активний:* this week focus is technical polish

**2. Romanian deadlift · 3×8 @ RPE 5**
- Weight ~60% normal working
- Tempo: 4-0-1-0 (very slow eccentric)
- Rest: 2 хв
- *Coaching focus:* hip hinge initiation, lumbar lock

**3. Goblet squat · 3×10 @ RPE 4**
- Dumbbell (light, 16-22kg)
- Focus on depth і controlled tempo
- Rest: 90 sec

### Mobility · 8 хв

- Pigeon pose · 90 sec per side
- 90/90 active stretching · 90 sec per side
- Cat-cow · 60 sec
- Foam roll quads + hamstrings · 4 хв

**Total session:** ~40 хв
**Volume:** 9 working sets (vs normal 13)

## Session 3 · Upper light + Mobility · Wednesday

### Warm-up · 10 хв

- Row machine · 5 хв easy
- Shoulder mobility · 3 хв (band pull-aparts, scapular CARs)
- Empty bar bench practice · 2 хв

### Main work

**1. Bench press · 3×8 @ RPE 5**
- Weight ~65% 1RM
- Tempo: 3-1-X-1 (slow lower, paused, up)
- Rest: 2 хв
- *Coaching focus:* scapular retraction, bar path stability

**2. Overhead press · 3×8 @ RPE 5**
- Weight ~55% normal working
- Tempo: 3-0-1-1
- Rest: 2 хв
- *Coaching focus:* lumbar lock, head position

**3. Dumbbell row · 3×10 each arm @ RPE 5**
- Tempo: 2-0-1-1
- Rest: 90 sec
- Focus: scapular retraction, no rotation through spine

### Mobility · 6 хв

- Thoracic spine stretches · 2 хв
- Pec stretches · 2 хв
- Wrist mobility · 1 хв
- Foam roll back · 1 хв

**Total session:** ~38 хв
**Volume:** 9 working sets

## Session 4 · Rest · Thursday

Same approach as Tuesday — walk або complete rest.

## Session 5 · Full body skill · Friday

**Purpose:** Practice movements з very light load, perfecting motor patterns. NOT conditioning.

### Warm-up · 8 хв

Generic activation + body weight movements

### Main work

**1. Deadlift practice · 5 sets × 5 reps @ RPE 4**
- Weight: ~50% normal working
- 60-90 sec rest between sets
- *Focus:* perfect setup, hip-shoulder synchrony

**2. Front squat practice · 4 sets × 5 reps @ RPE 4**
- ~50% normal
- *Focus:* upright torso, elbow position

**3. Strict push-ups · 3 sets × 10**
- Body weight
- Tempo: 3-1-1-0
- Focus: tight core, no hip drop

**4. Pull-up або lat pulldown · 3 sets × 8 @ RPE 5**
- Slow tempo, full ROM
- Focus: scapular initiation

**5. Plank holds · 3 sets × 45 sec**

**Total session:** ~45 хв
**Volume:** 17 working sets, but all light (RPE 4-5)

## Що відстежувати під час deload

### Signals що deload працює

- HRV trend ↑ (week 7 day 4-5 — peak)
- Sleep deeper (deep sleep ↑ 15-20 хв)
- Resting heart rate ↓ 2-3 bpm
- Motivation rebounds
- Joints / connective tissue stop aching
- Subjective energy 4-5 out of 5

### Signals що deload недостатній (продовжуй ще на тиждень)

- HRV stuck flat або declining
- Sleep still bad
- RHR stays elevated
- Persistent injury feelings
- Subjective energy < 3

### Coming back

After deload, Week 5 (or 7 if extended):
- Test 1RM if пропланований testing block
- Або start new mesocycle з 75-80% volume of previous peak
- Increase weight slowly first 2 weeks

---

## Nutrition during deload

- Calorie target: same або +100-150 ккал
- Protein: same as training week
- Carbs: stay normal — not «cutting calories because not training as hard»
- Water: same
- Alcohol: можна 1-2 glasses one evening max
- Sleep targets: ≥ 8 hours preferred

**Don't undereat during deload. Recovery requires fuel.**

---

## Common mistakes

❌ Skipping deload because «feeling fine»
❌ Treating deload as «cardio week» (adds stress)
❌ Reducing volume but keeping intensity (defeats purpose)
❌ 0% training (de-conditioning starts after 5+ days)
❌ Using deload as testing week (test post-deload)
❌ Drinking heavily during deload («I have time to recover»)
❌ Adding novel activities з skill demands (climb, rock-jump)

---

## Adjustments by user signal

### Coming off illness during this week:
- Sessions 1, 3, 5 → walk only
- Resume light training next week

### Travel during deload:
- Adapt to bodyweight version of each session
- Same RPE targets, different exercises

### Stress at work spike:
- Don't add training stress on top
- Compress to 2 sessions, more sleep
