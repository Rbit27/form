Billing &
contract
detail.

All enterprise contracts are annual. You can pay monthly (invoiced at the start of each month) or upfront annually (one invoice, 10% lower rate). Multi-year contracts (2yr, 3yr) are billed annually within the term — the discount applies to each year's invoice. We do not offer month-to-month enterprise billing. Invoicing via Stripe or ACH wire; PO number support included.
You purchase a seat block upfront at the start of each annual term. Seats can be added mid-year at your contracted rate — charged prorated to the next renewal. Seats cannot be reduced mid-term. At renewal, you true-up to actual peak usage (with a 10% buffer). If your seat count crosses a tier boundary at renewal, the new rate applies to your full seat count from that date.
Our SOC 2 Type II audit is in progress under Vanta continuous monitoring. Target completion is 12 months post-commercial launch. Enterprise-tier customers will receive a copy of the report under NDA upon completion. Starter and Growth customers can request a summary letter. In the interim, we can share our pen-test findings summary and Vanta compliance dashboard access under NDA for any active pilot.
The Data Processing Agreement is signed before any employee data flows into FORM — always, without exception. It covers GDPR Article 9 health data obligations, a DPIA for health-category processing, Standard Contractual Clauses for US–EU transfers, EU data residency election (Frankfurt or Dublin), and a sub-processor list with 30-day advance notice of changes. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom data processing terms. CCPA service provider addendum available on request.
Three pilot structures are offered. Free Pilot (standard): 90 days, up to 250 seats, no payment required, full Growth-tier feature access. No credit card on file. Default for all inbound prospects. Extended Free Pilot: 180 days, any seat count, designed for large-enterprise procurement cycles that need a longer evaluation window. Requires written founder approval — minimum 500-seat conversion commitment expected. Paid Pilot: 90 days at $1/seat/month, flat rate. Designed for accounts ≥ 500 seats where procurement policy requires an established billing relationship before budget approval. If you convert within 30 days of the pilot end, the full paid-pilot amount is credited against your Year 1 contract value. DPA is signed before any employee data enters FORM in all three structures. Success criteria are agreed upfront — you pay the annual contract only if the metrics hit.
Multi-year discounts apply as an annual reduction to your per-seat rate: 2-year contracts receive 15% off list price each year; 3-year contracts receive 25% off list price each year. These stack with the upfront-payment discount (additional 10% off when you pay each year's invoice upfront rather than monthly). Discounts are applied multiplicatively, not additively. Price floors are hard minimums — no approval level can override them: Starter tier floor is $6.00/seat/month (50% of $12 list); Growth tier floor is $4.50/seat/month (50% of $9 list); Enterprise tier floor is $4.00/seat/month (50% of $8 list reference rate). If a multi-year or stacked discount would take your effective rate below floor, the floor applies and the remaining discount is absorbed — not rolled into other concessions. Floor prices cannot be waived by a single approver; a commercial exception requires founder plus investor lead sign-off pre-Series A.
