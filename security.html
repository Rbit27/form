Security architecture
Multi-tenant isolation

Row-Level Security (RLS) on every table. tenant_id sourced from verified JWT claims — never client-supplied. Missing tenant_id returns zero rows, not all rows. Quarterly RLS policy audit.

Authentication

SAML 2.0 and OIDC SSO for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, OneLogin. SCIM v2 for automated provisioning. TOTP MFA enforced — SMS 2FA prohibited. Session max-age configurable per tenant.

Data residency

EU tenants routed to Supabase eu-central-1 (Frankfurt). AI inference via Anthropic Claude API — pseudonymised exercise context only, no PII in prompts. On-device CV processing — biometric keypoints never sent to cloud.

Incident response

P0–P3 classification. 72-hour GDPR Art. 33 supervisory authority notification SLA. 8-hour processor-to-controller notification for enterprise tenants. Full runbook at docs/INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md.

Backup & recovery

Supabase Point-in-Time Recovery (PITR) — 1-second granularity. Enterprise RTO ≤ 2 hours, RPO ≤ 1 hour. Cold backups to Cloudflare R2 with AES-256-GCM encryption and HMAC-SHA-256 manifest. Annual DR drill.

Privacy floor

HR never sees
individual data.

These seven commitments are non-negotiable, enforced at the data layer, and cannot be unlocked by any contract term — regardless of ARR. clinical-safety and compliance-officer hold veto power.

01
No individual user data for HR

Employer Admin Dashboard shows only aggregate metrics with k ≥ 5 anonymity floor. No row-level drill-down, ever.

02
No manager-level reports on individuals

Reporting hierarchy is not a data-access hierarchy. Managers cannot view direct-report fitness data.

03
Employee can revoke company association anytime

Offboarding an employee moves their account to a personal free tier — FORM does not delete user data on the employer's behalf without user consent.

04
Body composition data never aggregated to employer

Weight, body-fat percentage, and composition trends are strictly personal. No aggregate or trend is visible to HR.

05
ED-screening signals never surface to employer

Eating-disorder risk flags are clinical signals accessible only to the individual user and their opted-in care providers.

06
Mental health data stays private

Mood, stress self-reports, and recovery scores are personal wellness context — not employer visibility, not aggregate wellness reports.

07
No participation punishment mechanics

FORM does not provide non-participation lists, completion-rate tracking per individual, or any data that enables wellness-as-punishment program design.
