## Pitch 1 · TechCrunch / The Verge / ArsTechnica

**For:** Tech journalists covering AI startups, consumer tech, fitness products

**Subject options:**
- Why this Kyiv-based startup is taking a different angle on AI trainers
- Fitness app that admits what its AI can't do
- An AI personal trainer built on safety floor, not hype

```
Hi [Journalist Name],

[Personal hook — reference recent article they wrote on AI products or fitness apps]

Quick intro: FORM is an AI personal trainer that launches on App Store [date]. Built from Kyiv. The thing that might interest you:

Most "AI fitness apps" don't actually coach. They generate programs, track data, or surface content. We tested 8 — Fitbod, Whoop, Future, MacroFactor, Hevy, Apple Fitness+, Centr, Trainerize. Nobody coaches form, adapts to recovery, and respects users (no surveillance) in one product.

FORM does. Three differentiators:

1. Real-time form analysis via phone camera (8 base lifts, on-device, frames never leave your phone)
2. Programs adapt to HRV/sleep, not just calendar
3. Voice persona (Victor) with safety floor — ED-screening at onboarding, no surveillance language, no streak-shame

The story angle that might be interesting:

How a 1-founder + 14-AI-agent team из Kyiv is competing with $30/mo Whoop and $149/mo Future by combining what they can't.

Or:

The first AI fitness product that publishes what it WON'T do — and why that's the moat.

Available to chat at your convenience. Screen-share demo, exclusive embargo, founder interview — whichever works. I'm in UTC+3 but flex hours for media.

— George
form.coach
Press kit: form.coach/press (will be live launch week)
```

**Notes:**
- Tone: professional, not hyperbolic
- One angle per pitch — pick what fits the journalist's beat
- Reference their actual work — shows you did research
- Easy "no" path — don't pressure
- Add embargo offer for exclusivity

## Anti-patterns

- ❌ Mass-email pitch to 50 outlets
- ❌ «I'd love to chat sometime» (no specific ask)
- ❌ «Have you heard of X startup?» (assumes the conversation)
- ❌ Embargoed news with 48-hour expiry pressure
- ❌ Threaded follow-ups every 2 days
- ❌ Spreadsheet-tracker visible in email
- ❌ Generic «pitch deck attached» з no context
- ❌ «Time-sensitive opportunity» urgency tactics

---

## Reply expectations

- Cold pitch: 20-30% response rate (mostly nos)
- Warm intro: 60-80% response rate
- Specific reference to their work: 1.5x baseline
- Hard ask up front (specific request): higher quality replies, fewer

Plan для 8-12 quality conversations за month-1 launch, not 200 form-letters.

---

## Outlets to NOT pitch

- Generic «10 best startups of 2026» listicles
- Crypto / web3-focused outlets (wrong audience)
- Fitness influencer-investor combos
- Outlets with paid placement masquerading as editorial
