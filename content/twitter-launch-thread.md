## Tweet 4/14 (HRV-aware programming)

```
Most apps ignore your wearable data.

FORM reads HRV, sleep, RHR via HealthKit / Whoop / Oura — and adjusts.

HRV down 15% for 2 days? → swap heavy session for Zone 2.
Sleep < 6h? → reduce volume one set per lift.
Heavy day but recovery 80%? → push it.

Decisions, not noise.
```
