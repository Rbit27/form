Since the first email, the same two questions keep coming:

"What does FORM actually do?"  
"What's the AI part?"

Fair. Let me be specific.

---

**What FORM will do**

**1. Watch your form on eight lifts.**

Squat, deadlift, bench press, overhead press, barbell row, pull-up, hip hinge, lunge.

Using the camera on your phone, positioned at a consistent angle. Real-time feedback during the set. Victor tells you what's off — depth, tempo, rep completion — while you're still under load.

What it'll catch: incomplete range of motion, significant alignment deviations, missed reps.

What it won't diagnose: pain sources, medical conditions, joint pathology. We're not a physio. We're not a doctor. We don't pretend to be either.

**2. Adjust your program daily based on your readiness.**

Every morning, FORM reads your HRV and sleep data through Apple Health or a connected wearable. If your numbers say you're recovered, it loads the planned session. If they say you're not, it de-loads — shorter volume, lower intensity, same movement pattern.

This isn't a guess. It's standard periodization logic, applied automatically, with a brief explanation from Victor so you understand why today looks different from yesterday.

**3. Talk to you like a coach, not an app.**

That's Victor.

Victor doesn't say: "Amazing work! You're crushing it! 💪"  
Victor says: "Depth on set three. Reset before four."

Short. Specific. Based on what he actually saw, not on a generic encouragement algorithm.

---

**What FORM won't do**

**Medical diagnoses.** If it hurts, stop. See a professional. We'll never diagnose pain, recommend treatment, or suggest you push through injury signals.

**Calorie surveillance.** We log food for training context — protein, general intake — not to create a tracking relationship with eating. There's no daily calorie target, no "you're 200 calories over" alert, no macro scorecard. If you want detailed nutrition tracking, that's a different product. Intentionally.

**Before/after comparisons.** No body transformation marketing. No progress photos graded by appearance. Shame isn't a training tool. It doesn't work, and we're not using it.

**Guaranteed results.** Any app that guarantees specific outcomes is lying. We'll tell you what the program is designed to do, and we'll report honestly on what we learn from beta.

**Monetized behavioral data.** Your training data is yours. We don't sell behavioral profiles to advertisers. Full privacy policy at form.coach/legal (before launch: in the GitHub repo).

---

**What we don't know yet**

CV form analysis quality on real lifters, in real home gyms, under real lighting conditions.

We've built the architecture. We've modeled the pipeline. We haven't tested it at scale across different body types, lighting setups, camera angles, and training environments.

We'll know more when beta runs. When we do, you'll know — including if the accuracy isn't where it needs to be yet.

That's the deal.
